Health & Dental Plans

All full-time employees are eligible for medical, dental, and vision coverage starting the first of the month following their start date. Nimbus covers 85% of the premium for employee-only coverage and 70% for dependents across all three plan tiers below.

Retirement & 401(k)

Nimbus matches 100% of the first 4% of salary you contribute to your 401(k), vesting immediately — there is no vesting schedule. Enrollment is automatic at a 3% contribution rate unless you opt out or change your rate within your first 30 days.

Auto-enrollment starts at 3%, not 4%. To get the full match, increase your contribution to at least 4% in the benefits portal — auto-enrollment alone leaves 1% of match unclaimed.

Time Off & Leave

Nimbus uses a flexible PTO policy for exempt employees: no fixed accrual, no cap, manager-approved. Non-exempt employees accrue 15 days in year one, increasing to 20 days after three years of tenure. All employees receive 11 paid company holidays plus two floating holidays per year.

Glossary of Terms

Deductible — the amount you pay out of pocket before your insurance starts sharing costs.

HSA — Health Savings Account; tax-advantaged, used with HDHP plans, funds roll over every year.

FSA — Flexible Spending Account; tax-advantaged but generally use-it-or-lose-it each plan year.

Vesting — the point at which employer 401(k) contributions become fully yours to keep.

Out-of-pocket max — the most you'll pay in a plan year before insurance covers 100%.

Premium — the amount paid (by you and Nimbus together) to maintain your coverage.

PPO — Preferred Provider Organization; more flexibility to see out-of-network providers.

HDHP — High Deductible Health Plan; lower premiums, higher deductible, HSA-eligible.
