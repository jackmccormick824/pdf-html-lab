2. Your First Two Weeks

The first two weeks are deliberately light on output expectations and heavy on context-building. You'll pair with your onboarding buddy most afternoons, and mornings are yours for setup, reading, and small warm-up tasks.

Week 1

  • Laptop setup, VPN, and access provisioning (day 1)
  • Architecture overview sessions with each team lead
  • Ship your first "good first issue" pull request

Week 2

  • Shadow an on-call rotation (no pages, just observing)
  • Attend your first sprint planning as a full participant
  • 1:1 with your manager to set 30/60/90-day goals
No pager duty in week one or two. You'll be added to the on-call rotation starting week 5, after a shadow rotation and a dedicated incident-response walkthrough.
